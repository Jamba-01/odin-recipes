Crusless Grilled Cheese Odin Recipes

Quick and Simple crustless grilled cheese sandwich that is convenient to make for yourself or for the little ones!

INGREDIENTS

  • 8 sliced white sandwich bread : or any bread of your choice
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of softened butter : or as much as needed

STEPS

  1. Spread a thin layer of butter onto 1 side of each bread
  2. Using a round cookie cutter,press out round slces of chees. Lay 4 slices of bread on a work surface, buttered side down. Place a cheese round in the center of each slice.
  3. Top with remaining bread slices,buttered side up
  4. Center a sandwich sealer over a sandwich, press the sealer down and hold it in place to seal the edges, 15-30 seconds
  5. Pull the crust away from the sealer;discard. Repeat with the remaining sandwiches
  6. Preheat the air fryer to 280 degrees F, and enjoy
