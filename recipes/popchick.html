Popcorn Chicken Odin Recipes

Popcorn Chicken is a dish of bite-sized chunks of deep fried chicken that are sure to be a family favorite

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 2 cups of oil; or as much needed

STEPS

  1. mix flour, garlic powder, and black pepper together in a bowl. Beat eggs and lemon juice together in a seperate bowl
  2. Heat 2 inches of oil in a large pot over medium heat
  3. Dredge chicken pieces into the flour mix; shake off the excess. Dip into beaten egg, Lift up so excess eggs drips back into the bowl. Press into flour mix again to coat
  4. Lower pieces of chicken into hot oil in batches; fry until golden brown, 7-8 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain. Repeat remaining chicken pieces
