Quick and Easy! Homemade lasagna

INGREDIENTS

  • Beef : ground beef is fine or ground turkey
  • Spaghetti Sauce : store-bought is fine
  • Cheeses : cottage cheese, mozzarella, and parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings : dried parsely, sal, and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water : half a cup; pour around the edges of the baking dish before baking

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the meat of your choice, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Assemble the Lasagna
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and bake fore 10 minutes
